About Kitsamba Joshua

I'm Kitsamba Joshua, a Ugandan novelist and storyteller with a passion for crafting compelling and thought-provoking fiction. Drawing inspiration from everyday life, culture, and the human experience, I create stories that captivate readers and leave a lasting impression.

I attended Kireka Hill Infant School in Kireka, Ebenezer Primary School in Wakiso, Nabumali High School, and Hawthorne Scribner High School. My passion for storytelling inspires me to write engaging novels that explore the complexities of life and the human spirit.

Through my writing, I aim to inspire readers with stories rooted in resilience, hope, and transformation. My novels explore themes of love, loss, survival, destiny, and the strength it takes to rebuild after heartbreak. I believe that African stories deserve to be told—authentically, powerfully, and for the world to hear.

Join me on this literary journey as we celebrate African stories, one tale at a time.
