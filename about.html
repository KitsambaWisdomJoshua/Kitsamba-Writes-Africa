My Story

I'm Kitsamba Joshua, a Ugandan novelist and storyteller with a passion for crafting compelling and thought-provoking fiction. Drawing inspiration from everyday life, culture, and the vibrant experiences of African people, I write stories that resonate with readers across the continent and beyond.

I attended Kireka Hill Infant School in Kireka, Ebenezer Primary School in Wakiso, Nabumali High School, and Hawthorne Scribner High School. My passion for storytelling infused every stage of my education, as I discovered that stories are the most powerful vehicles for conveying truth, emotion, and meaning.

Through my writing, I aim to inspire readers with stories rooted in resilience, hope, and transformation. My novels explore themes of love, loss, survival, destiny, and the interconnectedness of human experiences. Whether examining the pain of grief or the joy of unexpected connections, my work celebrates the complexity and beauty of life.

My Mission & Vision

My Mission

To tell authentic African stories that celebrate our culture, inspire readers, and contribute to a global understanding of the African experience. I believe in the transformative power of literature to heal, educate, and unite people across borders.

My Vision

To establish Kitsamba Writes Africa as a beacon of African literary excellence, where authentic voices are celebrated, cultural heritage is honored, and stories inspire positive change in the world.

My Passion

I am dedicated to creating narratives that touch the heart, challenge the mind, and elevate African storytelling on the global stage. Every story I write is a gift to readers who seek meaning and connection.
