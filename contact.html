Frequently Asked Questions

How can I order your books?

You can purchase my novels through major online retailers. For bulk orders or special requests, please contact me directly through the form above.

Do you offer book signings or readings?

Yes! I participate in literary events and book signings. Contact me to discuss potential dates and locations for events in your area.

Can I submit my work for review?

I appreciate your interest. Please send a brief summary of your work along with details about your project, and I'll review it when time permits.

Are you available for interviews?

Absolutely! I'm happy to participate in interviews for blogs, podcasts, magazines, and other media outlets. Reach out with details about your publication.

How do I join your mailing list?

You can subscribe to my newsletter at the bottom of any page on this website. You'll receive updates about new releases, blog posts, and exclusive content.

Can I use your content for educational purposes?

For educational use of my content, please reach out with specific details. We can discuss licensing and permissions for your project.
